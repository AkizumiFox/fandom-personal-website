# Full Introduction｜Akizumi

Hi, I'm Akizumi.  
My fursona is a black fox, but sometimes I identify more as a lizard, and once in a while I feel like a white mouse.
I'm currently a fourth-year university student in Taipei, planning to extend my studies. I'm usually around Gongguan, and occasionally head out to Zhongli.

## Personality

I'm actually pretty shy, and I get nervous in places where I don't know many people.  
I also have severe face blindness (over three years of high school I could only remember about half of my class), so I genuinely fail to recognize a lot of people, and making friends has never come easily to me. In furry terms, I'm probably on the less socially adept side.

## Fursuits

Right now I have one full fox suit and one partial lizard suit.  
Honestly, one very practical reason I study hard is that I want to commission more suits from different studios. If you know me well, you've probably heard me wailing "I really want to buy a suit."
I generally prefer American-style suits over Japanese-style ones.

## Food

I love trying out restaurants — I want to give every kind of place a shot.  
Since my budget is limited, I mostly eat humble street food; but whenever time and money allow, I'm always happy to go eat with people.
I've worked my way through almost all of Taipei's Bib Gourmand listings, and I keep a [review list](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1P_i7hgWvSejgQaEGvacGZxL5GqCqXbqNflIs8T9o-Ts/edit?gid=0#gid=0).

## Fiction & Writing

I mostly read modern Taiwanese literature and literary fiction, and relatively little of other genres.  
I'm especially fond of flash fiction and short stories, and I'm practicing writing my own flash fiction.

My current goal is to write 50 pieces first, then consider self-publishing a short-story collection. Pieces I feel are mature enough gradually go up on [Fiction](/bookshelf).

## Hobbies

I usually play Texas Hold'em — I play tournaments almost every day, as a low-stakes recreational player, up around 100 USD.  
I also enjoy Japanese mahjong, bowling, and darts, and occasionally watch musicals.

## Wanting more friends

You're very welcome to come chat with me — about suits, food and fun, or fiction.  
If you're interested in what I study (computer science / economics), feel free to message me directly.
I'm not always the best at starting conversations, but I'll listen carefully and reply sincerely. If you're willing to share your story too, that would make me really happy.
